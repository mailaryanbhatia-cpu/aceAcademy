📋 Study Phase Guide

60+ Days Out

🌱 Foundation Phase

Cover all content units — use flashcards and unit tests. Don't rush; understanding > memorization.

30-60 Days Out

📖 Deep Review Phase

Re-do units you scored below 70% on. Start with FRQ practice. Review AP College Board rubrics.

14-30 Days Out

📝 Practice Test Phase

Take 1-2 full AP practice tests. Time yourself. Identify weak areas and drill those units.

7-14 Days Out

🎯 Intensive Review

Focus only on weak spots. Review FRQ rubrics daily. Do 1 past FRQ per day under timed conditions.

3-7 Days Out

🔄 Light Review

No new material. Review formulas, key terms, and major themes. One practice FRQ per day max.

Exam Week

😴 Rest & Ready

Sleep 8+ hours. Eat breakfast. Quick card review only. Arrive 30 min early with pencils and ID.
