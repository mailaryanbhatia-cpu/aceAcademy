The 4 Kinematics Equations

v = v₀ + at — relates velocity, acceleration, and time
Δx = v₀t + ½at² — relates displacement, initial velocity, acceleration, and time
v² = v₀² + 2aΔx — relates velocity, acceleration, and displacement (no time)
Δx = ½(v₀ + v)t — relates displacement, both velocities, and time (no acceleration)
