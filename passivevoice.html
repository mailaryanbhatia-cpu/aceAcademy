💡 Active Voice Tips

How to fix passive voice

  • Identify who or what is performing the action
  • Move the actor to the subject position
  • Replace the be-verb + past participle with a direct action verb
  • Example: "The test was taken by students" → "Students took the test"
  • Some passive voice is acceptable when the actor is unknown or unimportant
